About geocentrism mode

16th-century astronomer Tycho Brahe thought the Sun and Moon revolved around Earth, and everything else in the solar system revolved around the Sun.

His Tychonic system made the observed movement of the planets make sense mathematically, fit other theories at the time, and didn't violate the Christian belief that the Earth was the center of the universe.

All he was essentially doing was changing the frame of reference from the Sun's location to Earth's.
